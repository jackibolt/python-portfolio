About Jacki

Jacki is an enthusiastic freelance python and frontend developer, eager to build simple, clean and flexible solutions.

A life-long learner, Jacki started with a focus on frontend developemnt, learning the backbone of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Early projects included scratch-built portfolio sites for artists and video professionals, and a paid membership-based WordPress build to share coaching resources including video, PDF, image and text content. More recent work includes a Python/Flask build that scrapes college field hockey scores from more than 80 sources, normalizes match data and displays regularly updated results.

Jacki transitioned into Python development after a decade of coaching in the NCAA, always pushing boundaries with new innovative ways to get a little bit better each day. After stepping away from coaching, Jacki moved into an analyst role with a fast-growing medical tech company in Nashville, Tenn., continues to push forward adding developement tools to her skill stack, and choosing projects that feel challenging, valuable and exciting.
